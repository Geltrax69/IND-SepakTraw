# Purpose

The website primarily serves as the official portal of the SepakTakraw Federation of India.

Its major functions are:

- Publish federation notices
- Publish tournament information
- Publish national selection notifications
- Publish results
- Publish rules & regulations
- Government compliance (MYAS)
- RTI disclosures
- Election information
- Federation history
- Contact information
- Official downloadable documents

This is mostly an information/document management portal rather than an interactive web application.

# Technology Observations

The site appears to be built on WordPress.

Evidence includes:

- WordPress-style category URLs (`/category/events/`, `/category/rules/`)
- Media stored under `/wp-content/uploads/`
- Standard WordPress navigation and search structure
- PDF-based content publishing rather than dynamic modules. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
